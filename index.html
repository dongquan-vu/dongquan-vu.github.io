I am currently a postdoctoral researcher working at LIG – Laboratoire d’informatique de Grenoble; POLARIS Team – Performance Analysis and Optimization of Large Infrastructures and Systems.

My main research area is Artificial Intelligent. My current research interests lie at the intersection of learning, optimization and game theory with the scope of applications aiming towards operational research, resource management and multi-agent systems.

About me: I am currently working as a postdoctoral researcher at POLARIS team-LIG under the direction of Dr. Panayotis Mertikopoulos. I finished my PhD. in 2020 under the direction of Dr. Patrick Loiseau and Gérard Burnside, focusing on sequential learning in games and Colonel Blotto game. From January 2017 to December 2019, I worked as a research engineer at Nokia Bell Labs France, AAAID Lab. Prior to my Ph.D., I received my M.Sc. of Mathematics and Applications degree from Université Paris-Sud – Université Paris Saclay in 2016 and my B.Sc. of Mathematics from HCMC Vietnam National University in 2014. I have received First Prize Winner ROADEF Master Students Prize 2016 and Gaspard Monge Master Scholarship Program 2015.
